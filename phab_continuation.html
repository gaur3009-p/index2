Our Story

Born in Siliguri, Loved Across India

LUME LIVING began as a small passion project in Siliguri, with a simple belief: that the glow of a handmade candle can transform any space into a sanctuary.

What started in a home kitchen has grown into a beloved brand, shipping handcrafted soy wax candles, curated home decor, and bespoke gift hampers to customers across all 28 states of India.

Every product is 100% handmade using premium, eco-friendly materials. We believe in slow living, mindful creation, and the joy of gifting something truly personal.
